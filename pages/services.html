Thurmont Education offers 3 services:

Leadership & Employee Development

Grief Recovery Work

Community Education & Wellness

Tools utilized include but are not limited to:

  • Grief Recovery Method Private and Group Counseling
  • DISC/Meyer Briggs Personality Profile Testing
  • Individual Staff Assessment
  • Team Building exercises
  • Leadership Development Lectures based on needs assessment

    • Lectures available:
    • General Grief
    • Ancestral Grief
    • Generational Management
    • Stress Management
    • Team Building
    • Leadership for new managers
    • Communication in the workplace
    • Physician Communication
    • Mental health de-escalation for first responders
    • Cultural awareness lectures
    • Interfaith Dialogue

Fee Schedule

Financial support will be obtained through individual charitable donations and private fee-for-service payments. Scholarships are widely available, so please contact us to determine the cost. We will do our best to provide services for all in need.

  • Please confirm individual charges for all partners of Thurmont as fees vary for separate services.
  • Thurmont 7-session Grief Recovery Method program is an in-person, one-to-one program billed at $1400.
  • Group program is billed at $1200.
  • Individual leadership development and educational consultation costs will be determined on a sliding scale.
  • Lecture series are free to the community, with tailored lectures and consulting fees charged based on billable hours of instruction.
  • Thurmont Education has several scholarships available due to the generosity of our private donors.
